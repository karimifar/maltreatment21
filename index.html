Data presented in these maps are the estimated risk of maltreatment in communities across Texas. Data are not presented for communities with small child populations as these estimates are not reliable.

You can learn more about the data sources used to produce the estimates here. A full discussion of the metrics and guide on interpreting them can be found here .

Learn more about a community’s maltreatment risk by selecting it on the map and clicking “search” or by typing in the ZIP Code or county in the search bar
